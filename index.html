General Store is a neighbourhood grocery shop in Peckham, South London.

We sell cheese, bread, coffee, wine, beer, seasonal fruit and vegetables, and lots of store cupboard essentials.

We work with producers and suppliers who focus on the quality, integrity and provenance of their produce.
